Who We Are

Éire Gold was born from a deep love of Ireland's rich cultural heritage. Founded in Dublin, we create handcrafted jewellery that celebrates the beauty of Celtic tradition — from ancient knotwork to the iconic Claddagh symbol.

Every piece we make tells a story. Whether it's a gift for a loved one or a treasured keepsake, our jewellery is designed to last a lifetime and be passed down through generations.

Our Journey

2014

Founded in Dublin

Éire Gold opens its first studio in Dublin's creative quarter, with just three signature Celtic designs.

2016

First International Orders

Our Ireland Map Pendant goes viral online, bringing our jewellery to customers across 15 countries.

2019

Craft Award Winner

Éire Gold wins the Irish Craft Excellence Award for outstanding contribution to traditional Celtic jewellery.

2023

New Custom Service

We launch our bespoke custom jewellery service, allowing customers to create truly unique pieces.

2026

Growing Strong

Over 500 happy customers worldwide and still growing, with 150+ unique designs in our collection.
